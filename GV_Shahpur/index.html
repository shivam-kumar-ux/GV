About Us

Welcome to Gyanoday Vidyalaya

Gyanoday Vidyalaya is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to students from Nursery to 12th Grade. Our school is committed to nurturing young minds and shaping them into responsible citizens and future leaders.

Located in Shahpur, Bihar, we have been serving the educational needs of our community since 2018. Our experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and student-centric approach ensure that every child receives the best possible education.
