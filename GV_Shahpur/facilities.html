World-Class Facilities at Gyanoday Vidyalaya

We at Gyanoday Vidyalaya believe that a conducive learning environment is essential for the overall development of students. Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities that cater to the academic, physical, and creative needs of our students.

From well-equipped laboratories to a spacious library, from sports facilities to smart classrooms - we provide everything needed for a holistic educational experience.

Smart Classrooms

All our classrooms are equipped with smart boards and modern teaching aids to make learning interactive and engaging for students.

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories for practical learning and hands-on experience.

Computer Lab

Fully equipped computer lab with high-speed internet access for students to learn digital skills.

Library

Our well-stocked library has a wide collection of books, journals, and reference materials for all age groups.

Sports Facilities

Spacious playground and indoor sports facilities for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and other sports.
